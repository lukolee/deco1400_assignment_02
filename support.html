Using "What Can I Crochet?"

When on the browsing page this button will allow you to filter patterns by those that you can crochet based on the yarn that you have added to your catalogue.

To add yarn to your catalogue click the my yarn button in the header and add that which you have in your collection.

Using the Highlight Control Feature

To use the step mode simply click Begin Crochet Mode when previewing a pattern to enter the mode. The "back" and next buttons in the header allow you to go to the next or previous steps.

The exit button on the left hand side closes the view. The Highlight buttons advance the selected lines - allowing you to easily and simply see what steps you have already crocheted.
